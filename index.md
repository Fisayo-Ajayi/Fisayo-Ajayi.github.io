### 🛡️ 2. Financial Risk & Class-Imbalance Fraud Mitigation Suite
> **Core Objective:** Overcoming structural limitations in highly skewed transaction datasets to intercept credit card fraud and lower credit application defaults.

| Technical Component | Tools & Frameworks Deployed |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Algorithms** | Supervised Classification, Random Forests, Gradient Boosting |
| **Data Balancing** | Advanced Resampling, Precision-Recall (PR) Curve Optimization |
| **Evaluation Metrics**| F1-Score maximization over standard accuracy metrics |

*   **The Business Problem:** Financial services encounter consistent capital depletion from bad loans and unauthorized card charges. Developing classification engines is notoriously complex because fraudulent anomalies account for less than 1% of total dataset rows, meaning baseline machine learning algorithms default to ignoring the risk patterns to maintain artificial accuracy scores.
*   **The Engineering Solution:** Built automated preprocessing scripts to ingest highly skewed historical ledgers. Restructured the model training phase by optimizing for Precision-Recall (PR) AUC and F1-Scores rather than standard classification accuracy, ensuring fraud capture thresholds were accurately isolated.
*   **Commercial Business Value:** Delivers credit underwriting and monitoring teams an automated validation engine to catch transaction threats early, preserving capital without adding friction for authentic active subscribers.
